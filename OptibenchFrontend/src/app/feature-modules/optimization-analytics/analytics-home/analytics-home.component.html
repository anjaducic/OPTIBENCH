OPTIBENCH - Testing Optimization Algorithms

Select a problem from the dropdown list below, and view the algorithms that have been applied to solve it. Explore the history and details of the solving process, including histograms of the results.

The goal of optimization algorithms is to minimize or maximize an objective function, which represents a measure of solution quality. Different algorithms use various strategies to explore the solution space and find the optimal solution. Evaluating the performance of optimization algorithms requires testing them on diverse benchmark functions, which are well-known problems with known optimal solutions. Based on these tests, histograms, solution histories, and algorithm rankings can be generated, allowing for objective comparison of their performance. These analyses enable researchers to draw significant conclusions about the efficiency of different algorithms and identify their strengths and weaknesses in various situations. Additionally, such studies can help in identifying algorithms that are best suited for specific types of problems.
