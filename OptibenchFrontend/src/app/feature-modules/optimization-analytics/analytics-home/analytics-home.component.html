OPTIBENCH - Testing Optimization Algorithms

Select a problem from the dropdown list below, and view the algorithms that have been applied to solve it. Explore the history and details of the solving process, including histograms of the results.

The goal of an optimization algorithm is to minimize or maximize an objective function, which represents a measure of the quality of a solution. Different algorithms use different strategies to explore the solution space and find the optimal solution. Some algorithms use deterministic approaches, while others use stochastic methods such as genetic algorithms, simulated annealing, or particle swarm optimization. Evaluating the performance of optimization algorithms requires testing them on a variety of benchmark problems, which are well-known problems with known optimal solutions. This allows researchers to compare the performance of different algorithms objectively.
